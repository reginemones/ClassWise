FAQS

Answers to your most frequently asked questions.

It is not impossible for a scenario where the class have to elect a new president due to some unfortunate circumstances. To change the current president of the class inside the app,

Step 1. Contact the administrator of the ClassWise app.
Step 2. On the admin side of the application, click the button encircled in red to open the student list.
Step 3. Hit the change president button and select from the dropdown.
That's it!

There are three main reasons why you can't place a bid on an item.

You have pending transactions. Students must first clear their financial responsibilities before joining the auction event, so you must process all of your pending transactions in your dashboard.
To process a pending transaction,
Step 1. Go to your dashboard.
Step 2. Open pending transactions and hit the right headed arrow for each item then confirm.
That's it!

You cannot bid two consecutive times. A skilled bidder knows how to be patient. Do not rush and let someone outbid you before bidding again.

Your bid must be 20% higher. The bidding mechanics require the students to bid 20% higher than the last bid, meaning if the last bid is 150 wisecoins, the next bid must be atleast 180 wisecoins.

Forgetting your password is a normal thing. However, there is no way for students to reset their password on their own. You will need to contact the administrator and request to reset your password for you.

Step 1. Contact the administrator of the classwise app.
Step 2. Go to admin side of the application.
Step 3. On the dashboard, find or search the class of the student then click the encircled button as shown below.
Step 4. Find the student's name on the list then in the actions column you will find the settings button to reset the student's password.
That's it!

On the dashboard of every student, there 2 different transaction histories.

Personal transaction history. Students may want to track their personal transactions so that they know where their money went and came from.
To view your personal transaction history,
Step 1. Go to your dashboard.
Step 2. Under My Activity, open transaction history.
That's it!

Class transaction history. A transaction history for the class is a must to provide transparency and ensure fairness.
To view the class transaction history,
Step 1. Go to your dashboard.
Step 2. Under Class, open class transaction history.
That's it!
